Cut, copy and delete chips

1. Go to the select mode().

2. Drag regions to besiege chips. The chips are now selected.

3. Right click on the score to show the context menu. Please select item you'd like to do.

Remarks

  • You can "Paste" chips what are "Cut"-ed or "Copy"-ed. See next chapter in detail.

Paste chips

Right click where you'd like to paste. The context menu is shown. Select "Paste chip(s)" to paste.

Remarks

  • If you did a paste by clicking Menu bar-[Edit]-[Paste], the chips are pasted where the bottom area of displayed score.
